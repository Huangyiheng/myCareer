The Following Information

You May Concerned











  • HOW TO APPLY FOR PARAL TEACHER?
    • 1.   Sign up to create an account.
    • 2.  Complete a simple form and submit your application.
    • 3.  Our staff will contact with you and send some materials to you.
    • 4.  With given materials prepare a short online demo lesson. Have an interview with our staff.
    • 5.  Settle your own schedule and get access to additional information.
    • 6.  Get training and attend to the real class with our current teacher.
    • 7.  Sign your contract and start teaching!
  • How much can I earn?
    • Depends on your class hours, Our staff will give a specific wages instruction while having an interview.
  • What kind of commitment is there?
    • The 6-month minimum commitment is required, But during that time, you can arrange your own work schedule.
  • Do I need to speak Chinese?
    • No Chinese is required. We want to create full English immersion for our students.


If you have any questions, contact us at teach@maxwisdomglobal.com
